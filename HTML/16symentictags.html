Semantic HTML

HTML tags can be categorized into two types based on semantics in HTML. They are:

Semantic Tag

The tags which accurately describe their purpose and describe the type of their content are called semantic tags.

Some Examples of semantic tags are - <h1>,<h6>,<form>,<table>

Non-Semantic Tags

HTML non-semantic tags do not have a specific meaning or purpose. They are used to create general-purpose containers for content without providing any additional meaning or context.
